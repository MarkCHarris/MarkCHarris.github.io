Analyzing Food Deserts with Regression, Classification, and Geographical Data

View on GitHub

Collaborated with two colleagues using Git to analyze food deserts in Chicago. Our goal was to make recommendations for providing access to healthy foods within food deserts by means of local markets or mobile food trucks.
